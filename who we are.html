Who We Are?

We are the DORSU Raptors Esports Club, established in 2024 and proudly affiliated with Estudyante Esports of Dark League Studios. Our mission is to cultivate a dynamic community of gamers at Davao Oriental State University, where players of all skill levels can come together to compete, learn, and grow.

At DORSU Raptors, we believe in the power of teamwork, dedication, and sportsmanship. Our club provides a supportive environment that encourages members to develop their gaming skills, participate in exciting tournaments, and connect with fellow enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting your gaming journey, we welcome you to join us as we soar to new heights in the world of esports!

Our club is committed to hosting GAB-licensed tournaments, ensuring fair play and professionalism in every competition. We celebrate the diverse backgrounds of our members, fostering an inclusive atmosphere that inspires creativity and collaboration. As we build our legacy, we are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of competitive gaming while having fun and making lasting friendships along the way. Join the DORSU Raptors, where every gamer has the chance to shine.
