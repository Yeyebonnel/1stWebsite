Welcome to DOrSU RAPTORS

An esports club where the fiercest gamers of Davao Oriental State University unite to compete, excel, and conquer the world of esports! Get ready to level up with the ultimate gaming community.

Who We Are?

We are the DORSU Raptors Esports Club, established in 2024 and proudly affiliated with Estudyante Esports of Dark League Studios. Our mission is to cultivate a dynamic community of gamers at Davao Oriental State University, where players of all skill levels can come together to compete, learn, and grow.

What we Offer?

We bring you exciting esports tournaments, live streams, the latest gaming news, and a community where players and fans come together to enjoy the best in competitive gaming.
