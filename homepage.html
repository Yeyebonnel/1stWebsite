Who we are?

Teambangan Bakery was born from a love for baking and a desire to share the joy of homemade treats. Founded by a team of passionate bakers, we blend traditional recipes with modern flavors, creating a welcoming space where every loaf, pastry, and dessert is crafted with care and creativity.
