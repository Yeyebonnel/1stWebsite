Welcome to DOrSU RAPTORS


An esports club where the fiercest gamers of Davao Oriental State University unite to compete, excel, and conquer the world of esports! Get ready to level up with the ultimate gaming community.

Who We Are?


We are the DOrSU Raptors Esports Club formerly known as DOrSU Stallions, established in 2024 and proudly affiliated with Estudyante Esports of Dark League Studios. Our mission is to cultivate a dynamic community of gamers at Davao Oriental State University, where players of all skill levels can come together to compete, learn, and grow.


What We Offer?

We bring you exciting esports tournaments, live streams, the latest gaming news, and a community where players and fans come together to enjoy the best in competitive gaming.



TOURNAMENTS AND COMPETITIONS

Host exciting esports tournaments across popular games, providing opportunities for players of all levels to compete.





LIVE STREAMING AND REPLAYS

Enjoy live broadcasts of esports events and catch up on replays and highlights of the most thrilling moments.






GAMING NEWS AND COMMUNITY

Stay updated with the latest news in esports, and connect with fellow gamers through forums and discussions.
