At DOrSU, we proudly host a range of competitive esports tournaments, providing our students with the unique opportunity to showcase their talents while competing against peers from other universities. These exhilarating events not only foster a spirit of healthy competition but also enhance participants' visibility in the gaming community. Students can aspire to win exciting prizes, earn scholarships, and gain invaluable experience that opens doors to future opportunities within the dynamic esports industry.

Our Coaching and Training Programs are meticulously crafted to elevate the skills of aspiring esports athletes.
